About Us

The Robotics And Innovation club was established (officially) in January 2010. The Titans, the team for Robocon from College of Engineering Roorkee was formed on 15/9/2007, by handful of budding first yearlings. The long journey of officialising the club started in 2008-09 but it was until Jan. 2010 that the official recognition was granted. At present the club is under the able guidance of Dr. Kamal Kapoor who is the Co-Convener of the club.
